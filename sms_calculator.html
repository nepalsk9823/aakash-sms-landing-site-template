How to Use the SMS Length Calculator

Our SMS Length Calculator is an easy-to-use tool that helps you analyze your text message by counting characters, identifying character types, estimating costs, and more. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  • Step 1:
    Enter your message by typing it in or pasting it. You can also select from pre-made templates for convenience.
  • Step 2:
    You can see the length, sms count and remaining at the end of the sms field.

Why to Use the SMS Length Calculator

  • Instantly check the total character count in your SMS.
  • Determine how many message segments your text will be split into.
  • Identify GSM characters, Unicode characters, and GSM symbols that require Unicode encoding.
  • Estimate the cost of sending your SMS.
  • Optimize your text by adjusting characters to minimize segmentation and extra charges.
