Introducing Marketing Link Feature in Bulk SMS; Track Conversion, Clicks & More

Do you wish to attach your important links to your text SMS and send it to your target audiences? Well, that is a great marketing strategy, given that SMS messages have a whopping 98% Open rate and 45% Response rate. In this digital age, leading your audiences to your product or service is a hell of a task. But no more!

We are thrilled to introduce an innovative and highly effective addition to our Bulk SMS solution: SMS Marketing Link & Tracking. This means you no longer have to guess how your recipients interact with your sms. Our tool keeps track of their every move.

In this blog, you will get to know what sms marketing link really is, why this feature is a game-changer, how it works, how to use it, different businesses that can benefit, and more.

So, let’s embark on a journey together to learn the ins and outs of the SMS marketing link feature and see if it is useful for your business.

What is the Marketing Link Feature?

Some estimates suggest inaccurate phone data can cost businesses up to 12% of their revenue. This is why it’s important to find and verify phone number lists.

Plus, the cost per contact, or each interaction with a customer, can range from $3 to $7. This means, when you find real phone numbers for your marketing and sales campaigns, you can save on operational costs and increase agents’ efficiency and productivity. 

Reaching real people also lets you enhance customer satisfaction, comply with regulations, improve the reliability of your call center metrics, and maintain a positive reputation for your business. In this blog, we’ll explore methods and databases call centers can use to find and verify phone number lists—so you can get the right person on the phone, every time. 

Key takeaways:

  • Accurate phone number lists are crucial for call centers to save operational costs and improve agent efficiency. 
  • Call centers can find real phone numbers through third-party vendors, web scraping, existing customer databases, and collaborative partnerships. Each method has its benefits and challenges. 
  • CloudTalk’s virtual and VoIP phone numbers help call centers make a great first impression.

Reliable Databases for Finding Real Phone Numbers

Here is a brief overview of top databases that can help you find real phone numbers to use for sales and customer support initiatives.

Whitepages Pro

Whitepages Pro is a reliable database for finding phone numbers. It identifies the number as active or invalid and provides additional data points, such as name, address, and demographic information about the number owner. It also has a robust set of fraud detection tools that minimize potential fraud risks by assessing a number’s activity and history.

Experian Data Quality

Experian Data Quality software ensures the accuracy and validity of contact information. It sources data from over 200 telecommunications providers to make sure they’re correct and helps businesses maintain clean and reliable contact data. This helps call centers improve their operational efficiency and customer engagement.

Neustar

Neustar is an advanced phone number verification service provider ensuring contact information is accurate and up-to-date. This helps businesses enhance their communication strategies and maintain compliance with regulatory standards.
