What is Bulk SMS ?

Bulk SMS is a powerful tool for sending large volumes of messages quickly and efficiently to mobile phones. Commonly used for alerts, reminders, marketing, and communication, it benefits businesses, banks, media, and consumer brands by enabling fast and direct customer engagement.

With higher open and interaction rates compared to other marketing methods, Bulk SMS connects businesses to a large audience instantly.

It’s cost-effective, works on any mobile phone without additional apps, and ensures seamless delivery across telecom networks, making it an ideal offline marketing solution for growing customer reach with minimal investment.
