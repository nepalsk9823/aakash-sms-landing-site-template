All-in-One SMS Solution

Whether you're communicating with a large student body or individual faculty members, AakashSMS keeps your entire educational community connected seamlessly.

Admission Alerts

Effective communication is key to a smooth admission and enrollment process in the education sector. SMS ensures students receive timely updates, reducing delays and enhancing their experience. Instant notifications keep applicants informed and help institutions manage admissions efficiently.

  • Notify students about their application progress instantly
  • Send reminders to submit necessary documents on time
  • Confirm fee payments with quick SMS alerts
  • Share important details about orientation and schedules

Exam Results

Timely communication about exams is crucial for students and parents to stay informed and prepared. SMS ensures quick and reliable updates, making academic planning smoother and more efficient.

  • Notify students about exam dates, times, and venues
  • Share results instantly, reducing wait times
  • Send subject-wise feedback for improvement
  • Inform parents and students about grade promotions

Events & Emergency Notifications

Keeping students, parents, and staff informed about important events and emergencies is essential for a safe and well-organized academic environment. SMS ensures instant and reliable communication.

  • Send invites and reminders for school events and activities
  • Share last-minute updates or schedule changes
  • Alert students and parents about urgent situations like campus closures
  • Provide quick safety instructions and emergency response details

Sending bulk text messages in Nepal is simple and convenient with Aakash SMS. Our platform is designed to help businesses, organizations, and individuals reach thousands of people instantly through a reliable SMS gateway. Whether you’re promoting a product, sending notifications, or sharing important updates, you can easily manage your campaigns online with just a few clicks.

  1. Create an Account – Sign up on the Aakash SMS platform and log in to your dashboard.
  2. Upload Contacts – Add your recipient list by uploading a CSV/Excel file or entering numbers directly.
  3. Compose Your Message – Write your SMS content with the required information, ensuring it fits within the character limit.
  4. Choose Sender ID – Select or apply for a custom sender ID (if applicable) to represent your brand.
